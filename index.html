Meet our team

Project #1: KMLA Forge

We founded a collaborative workspace for teenage engineers, renovating an abandoned school space. In KMLA Forge, students can craft and envision anything they imagine!

Project #2: BUZ Aerospace

We design and launch solid-fuel rockets. Currently, we are designing a 400N spec I-Class Rocket. Also in development on BUZ Rocket Engine 2.0, 800N spec. Starting with simple rocket fuel experiments, we are now looking forward to the Launch of Silver Snoopy, a 1m long reusable metal rocket, aiming to launch in December!
