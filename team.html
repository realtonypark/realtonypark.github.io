Our founding

In June 2022, six of the best engineers of KMLA gathered to start something never done in the school's history.


Junseok, KMLA's brightest idea bank, Seongwoo, KMLA's best mechanical engineer, Jinho, KMLA's best computer engineer, Seunghyeon, KMLA's best AI engineer, Yongwook, KMLA's best scientist, and Geon, KMLA's best machinist, dreamt of KMLA's first ever Rocket-Lab.

Growth & beyond

Our initial progress attracted the attention of tens of our school's juniors, whose hearts brimmed with the same passion. Together, our ideas grew bigger; our initial mission of a private rocket lab transformed into a multi-purpose melting pot of ideas, and our experiments with rocket fuel turned into full-scale class I thrust tests.

Today, with our newest member Mr. Jeon and investments from the school, we continue to grow and spread our mission of leaving a legacy in KMLA's history.
